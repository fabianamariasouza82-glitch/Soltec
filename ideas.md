# Design Strategy - Soltécnicas

## Chosen Design Approach: Professional & Trustworthy

**Design Movement:** Modern Corporate with Technical Precision

**Core Principles:**
1. **Trust Through Clarity** - Clean layouts, legible typography, and direct communication build confidence in technical expertise
2. **Professional Authority** - Blue as the primary color conveys stability and professionalism in the electrical services industry
3. **Functional Elegance** - Minimal ornamentation, purposeful spacing, and strategic use of whitespace
4. **Visual Hierarchy** - Clear distinction between primary CTAs, secondary information, and supporting content

**Color Philosophy:**
- **Primary Blue** (#003366): Deep, professional blue that represents trust, stability, and technical expertise
- **Accent Orange** (#ff6600): Warm, energetic accent for CTAs and highlights - conveys action and reliability
- **Neutral Grays** (#f5f5f5, #666666): Clean backgrounds and readable text
- **White**: Primary background for clarity and professionalism

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric hero section with image on right, content on left
- Horizontal card-based service showcase
- Two-column layouts for "About" section
- Full-width sections with alternating backgrounds (white/gray)
- Generous whitespace between sections

**Signature Elements:**
1. **Blue electrical accent lines** - Subtle decorative lines that suggest circuits/electrical work
2. **Professional photography** - High-quality images of electricians in action
3. **Orange CTA buttons** - Distinctive, action-oriented buttons throughout

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth hover effects on cards and buttons
- Subtle transitions between sections
- Clear focus states for accessibility
- Responsive design that maintains hierarchy on mobile

**Animation:**
- Fade-in animations on scroll for service cards
- Smooth button transitions (100-150ms)
- Subtle hover scale effects on interactive elements
- No excessive motion - professional and restrained

**Typography System:**
- **Display Font**: Bold, modern sans-serif for headings (Poppins or similar)
- **Body Font**: Clean, readable sans-serif (Inter or similar)
- **Hierarchy**: H1 (3xl), H2 (2xl), H3 (xl), Body (base)

**Brand Essence:**
*Soltécnicas: Professional electrical solutions that businesses trust for safety, quality, and reliability.*
- **Personality**: Trustworthy, Professional, Reliable, Expert

**Brand Voice:**
- Headlines: Direct, confident, benefit-focused
- CTAs: Action-oriented, clear next steps
- Microcopy: Professional but approachable
- Example lines: "Segurança Elétrica que Sua Empresa Merece" / "Confiabilidade em Cada Projeto"

**Signature Brand Color:** Deep Professional Blue (#003366)

**Logo Concept:** Bold geometric symbol representing electrical circuits or a lightning bolt within a shield - conveys both technical expertise and protection.
